## 4) Visual & Interaction Spec (Plain Language)

**Layout & Spacing Philosophy**  
- Generous spacing, strong alignment  
- Clear vertical rhythm  
- No visual noise or decorative elements

**Typography Feel**  
- Modern, elegant, highly legible  
- Clear hierarchy: confident headings, calm body text  
- Typography is the primary design signal

**Color Palette**  
- **Base:** Deep charcoal / near-black (not pure black)  
- **Text:** Soft off-white / light gray  
- **Accent:** Muted purple tones (used sparingly for links, emphasis, and CTA states)  
- No bright or saturated colors

**Buttons & Links**  
- Minimal, text-forward  
- Subtle hover states (color shift within purple spectrum, light opacity change)  
- No heavy borders or shadows

**Motion Principles**  
- Smooth scrolling  
- Gentle fade or slight vertical reveal on section entry  
- Motion should feel fluid, slow, and intentional

**Mobile Behavior**  
- Fully responsive  
- Same hierarchy and spacing philosophy  
- CTA always reachable but never persistent or intrusive
